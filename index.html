Meet Adam Caver

Graduate Jeweler & Gemologist crafting purposeful, personal pieces that honor your milestones.

Adam began shaping metal and stone in 2006, earning Applied Jewelry Arts credentials from GIA. With advanced stone-setting expertise, he combines design intuition with meticulous craftsmanship.

From fresh concepts to heirloom redesigns, every project is collaborative. Share your story, choose your stones, and watch a custom piece come to life—built to be worn, cherished, and passed down.
